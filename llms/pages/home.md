Primary audience: builders, technical evaluators, and production sponsors trying to understand Volant Labs and Vellis.

## Summary

Volant Labs publishes open graph-native infrastructure for AI-native work. The homepage introduces Vellis as an open-source context graph engine for AI agents: a typed, locally owned system that MCP-capable agents can query and change through one explicit contract. Vellis can serve as a personal context graph, a team knowledge substrate, or agent memory infrastructure.
Its Start Here routes lead directly to the Vellis Engine, the Volant Labs Thesis, and the open community.

## Use This Page For

- Understanding the relationship between Volant Labs, Vellis, Perspectives, Community, and Volant Partners.
- Routing users to the Vellis quick start, the thesis, recent Perspectives posts, or production support.
- Establishing the public posture: open lab first, Vellis first project, governed production operations through Volant Partners.

## Key Claims

- Vellis models real work as explicit knowledge carried by typed anchors, facts, links, and schemas.
- The site emphasizes controlled change and recoverable state through snapshots, restore, ledger replay, and rebuilt-state verification.
- Volant Labs is the open lab of Volant Partners.
