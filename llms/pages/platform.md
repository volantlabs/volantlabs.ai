# Platform

Canonical URL: https://volantlabs.ai/platform.html
HTML path: /platform.html
Primary audience: production sponsors and teams that need governed operations around open Vellis patterns.

## Summary

The Platform page explains the path from open Vellis patterns to Kesher, Volant Partners' governed platform for production operations. Kesher adds write controls, audit traces, approval paths, and operating support without positioning Vellis as a gated trial.

## Use This Page For

- Distinguishing open, self-managed Vellis from governed production implementation in Kesher.
- Routing teams that need audit, operating controls, and support to Volant Partners.
- Avoiding the impression that Vellis alone operationalizes regulated workflows.

## Key Claims

- Vellis remains open, runnable, inspectable, and portable.
- For production use, authentication, policy, deployment, and operating controls remain the builder's responsibility unless the team adopts a governed platform and support model.
- Kesher is Volant Partners' governed platform; Volant Partners supplies implementation support.
